About the artist

My name is Estera. I come from Croatia, but live in the United Kingdom where I work in a dog rescue centre. Dogs have been my passion since I got my first dog Luna. My other passion is painting. I have been painting since I was a little girl following my father’s steps. Therefore, I made an only possible decision and that is to paint dogs. Mostly I enjoy doing abstract paintings of dogs, but I do very much enjoy making realistic ones as well. Hope you like my paintings as much as I liked making them.
